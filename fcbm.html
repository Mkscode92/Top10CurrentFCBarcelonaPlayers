FC Bayern Munich: Top 10 Best Currently Playing Players

Fans across the world voted to determine which FCBM player they thought was the best, and the results display who have the highest rankings!

PUBLISHED MAR 4, 2024

In celebration of FC Bayern Munich's 124th creation anniversary and their recent Bundesliga triumph, fans were asked to
send in their votes of who their top players are, and the results were tallied in the FCBM top 10 poll from reddit's r/BayernMunich.

With a large variety of players to choose from, it's no surprise that many voters struggled to pick the best. However, some
players have stood out more to fans and enjoyers, whether through their skills, personality, or much more. The team wouldn't
be the same without them.

10

Alphonso Davies

FCBM's superstar left-back. His blistering pace a nightmare for defenders, allowing him to surge forward in attack or effortlessly recover in defense. Davies couples this with incredible dribbling skills, making him dangerous when cutting inside. His crossing and passing accuracy make him a constant creator of goalscoring chances. Beyond his offensive threat, Davies is a solid defender with a relentless work ethic that perfectly embodies Bayern Munich's winning mentality.

9

Joshua Kimmich

FCBM's heartbeat of the midfield. His versatility is unmatched - he commands the game as a defensive midfielder, breaking up play and dictating the tempo, but can equally excel as a right-back. His passing range is exceptional, allowing him to pick out teammates with laser-like precision. Kimmich's intelligence and tactical awareness make him a leader on the pitch. His tireless work ethic and commitment to excellence translate directly into Bayern Munich's success.

8

Mathys Tel

FCBM's teenage French sensation. His pace and dribbling ability are electrifying, constantly threatening defenses. He's a clinical finisher, possessing a composure beyond his years in front of goal. Tel's versatility is a major asset; he can play as a striker or a winger. His raw talent, tactical intelligence, and hunger for goals make him a perfect fit for Bayern's relentless attacking style. He always brings a tremendous impact whenever he's brought onto the pitch.

7

Mathijs de Ligt

FCBM's formidable Dutch defender. He has been a rock at the heart of Bayern Munich's defense. He's known for his physical strength, exceptional tackling, and superb aerial ability, allowing him to dominate duels. De Ligt's positional awareness and tactical intelligence make him a commanding presence. His leadership qualities further strengthen Bayern's backline. Moreover, his ability to build up play from the back adds another dimension to Bayern's attacks. He also has a tendency to make tremendous goal line saves.

6

Leroy Sané

FCBM's brilliant German winger. His blistering speed allows him to tear past defenders and create space for himself or his teammates. Sané's dribbling is mesmerizing, and his ability to cut inside and unleash powerful shots makes him a prolific goalscorer. He has a keen eye for a pass and his creativity in the final third unlocks even the most stubborn defenses. He is currently having a monster season, and has many assists and goals to his name. Sané's dynamic style and attacking flair make him a vital attacking weapon for Bayern Munich.

5

Thomas Müller

FCBM's veteran star. His uncanny ability to find and exploit space between defenders makes him a constant goal threat. Müller's movement off the ball is unmatched, always finding positions where he can influence the game. THis is caused him to have broken multiple records, including having some of the highest assist numbers in the history of the sport. His tactical intelligence, tireless work rate, and ability to link play seamlessly with teammates make him the heart of Bayern's attack.

4

Jamal Musiala

FCBM's young German attacking sensation. He has become a key figure for Bayern Munich, with his exceptional dribbling skills, close control, and ability to ghost past defenders which make him a nightmare for opposing teams. Musiala possesses remarkable vision, finding incisive passes to unlock defenses and create scoring chances. He's incredibly versatile, operating as a winger, attacking midfielder, or even a second striker. Musiala's goal threat and tactical intelligence make him a vital asset to Bayern's relentless attacking style, and his potential seems limitless.

3

Kim Min-jae

FCBM's center-back powerhouse. He has become a formidable anchor for Bayern Munich's defense. His nickname "The Monster" is fitting; he's physically dominant, winning aerial duels and crunching into tackles. Kim's composure on the ball allows him to play out from the back with precision. His reading of the game is superb, often nullifying out opposition attacks before they develop. Kim Min-jae's presence solidifies Bayern's defense, making him a significant asset to one of the world's top clubs.

2

Manuel Neuer

FCBM's goalkeeping icon. His dominance in the penalty area, paired with his anticipation and speed off his line, make him a "sweeper-keeper," extending his influence far beyond the goal. Neuer's exceptional foot skills allow him to initiate attacks with pinpoint passing and distribution. His ability to nullify threats, command his defense, and even take penalties solidifies him as an irreplaceable leader for Bayern Munich. Neuer's unique blend of traditional goalkeeping with aggressive, proactive play makes him one of the greatest goalkeepers of all time.

1

Harry Kane

FCBM's English super-signing. He is the replacement striker for Robert Lewandowski that FCBM desperately needed, if not better. He's a clinical finisher and prolific goalscorer, with lethal instincts inside the box and powerful shots from distance. His link-up play is exceptional, allowing him to drop deep and creatively feed teammates. Kane's leadership and work ethic are an immediate boost for the team. His ability to score from a variety of positions and create chances for others makes him the perfect spearhead of Bayern's attack. He has truly been thriving at FCBM, with a jaw-dropping 35+ goals and assists in just 32 matches in his first season.
